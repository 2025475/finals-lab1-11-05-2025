My Life as a Grade 11 ICT Student in KCP

My life as an ICT student was great, filled with ups and downs, and a lot of memorable moments.

Click to read more about my journey.

Each day brought something new and exciting, and I learned to embrace every challenge with a positive mindset.

Choosing ICT Over STEM

Originally, my first option was to take STEM, but sadly, my grades didn't reach the required level. So, I decided to take ICT instead. It turned out to be a blessing in disguise!

Did you know? ICT stands for Information and Communication Technology, a field that's growing rapidly in today's digital age!

New Environment, New Experiences

The environment was different for me. My classmates were unique and unlike what I expected. However, I was quick to adapt and even found myself enjoying the differences.

Making Friends and Memories

The fun part was meeting new people. We bonded over common interests like:

  • Playing games together
  • Exploring new places to eat
  • Collaborating on projects
Favorite Games
- Mobile Legends, Valorant, GTA , Minecraft, and Codm.
Best Food Spots
- Canteen, Local Cafes, Pancake house ,or Fastfood Restaurants.

Memorable Projects

One of the best projects was the Music Video assigned by ma'am Dahlia. We enjoyed it immensely because we got to brainstorm creative ideas and made some fantastic memories along the way.

We also had a blast working on the Arduino booth, especially since we collaborated with our ate's and kuya's, which made it even more special.

Bittersweet Endings

As the school days were coming to an end, I felt a tinge of sadness knowing that I might not see some of my friends again. But that's the beauty of life; it's always about new beginnings.

So, here I am, ready to start a new chapter in my Grade 12 ICT journey.
